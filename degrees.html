DEGREES

SAVBOR ranking system uses an advancement system of colored belts similar to that of most martial arts. The different levels of belts are:
BASIC LEVEL
White Belt, Yellow Belt, Orange Belt, Green Belt
ADVANCED LEVEL
Blue Belt, Purple Belt, Brown Belt, Black Belt
MASTER LEVEL
Black Belt 1st degree
Black Belt 2nd degree
Black Belt 3rd degree
Black Belt 4th degree
